Systems & Software for High Performance Computing Research Laboratory (SysHPC)

Welcome to our laboratory website! We conduct foundational research in computer systems, software, and architectures across high performance computing (HPC) systems, parallel and distributed cloud platforms, and emerging post-Moore systems, including quantum computing platforms.
Our work delivers novel runtime systems, schedulers, and resource managers, that are largely open-sourced to advance performance, energy efficiency, sustainability, productivity, and cost-effective computing at scale.

We are always excited to work with highly motivated students and researchers. Please look at out publications and research areas for more information. Reach out to us if you think your interests are a good match.
